Dvievācika-ādi Pavāraṇā

The Pavāraṇā performed by uttering the Pavāraṇā statement once is 'Ekavācika Pavāraṇā'. The Pavāraṇā performed by uttering the Pavāraṇā statement twice is 'Dvievācika Pavāraṇā'. The Pavāraṇā performed by bhikkhus who have observed the Rains Retreat together, as a group, by uttering it once, is 'Samānavassika Pavāraṇā'. These methods of performing Pavāraṇā are necessary in times when there are many bhikkhus. As there are not so many bhikkhus at present, occasions for performing these Pavāraṇās do not arise. Therefore, they are not presented here. Those who wish to know should refer to the Mahāvagga Pavāraṇakkhandhaka.
