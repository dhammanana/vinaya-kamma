Authorization to be Separate from the Triple Robe

After the expiration of the privileges of the Kathina, if one stays even for one night separate from the triple robe, it is an offense of Expiation involving Forfeiture (nissaggiya pācittiya). Therefore, if a bhikkhu has a triple robe that has been determined (adhiṭṭhāna), he must take them wherever he goes. A bhikkhu who is unable to take the triple robe with him due to some infirmity should obtain the ticīvarena avippavāsa sammuti (Authorization to be separate from the triple robe). For a bhikkhu who has received this, there is no offense in staying separate from the triple robe.

The sick bhikkhu should approach the Community, arrange his upper robe over one shoulder, pay homage at the feet of the senior bhikkhus, sit in the squatting position, and having saluted the Community, speak this sentence:

Ahaṃ bhante! gilāno, na sakkomi ticīvaraṃ ādāya pakkamituṃ. So’haṃ bhante saṅghaṃ ticīvarena avippavāsasammutiṃ yācāmi. Dutiyampi -pe- tatiyampi -pe-

The Procedural Motion

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, ayaṃ (tisso) bhikkhu gilāno, na sakkoti ticīvaraṃ ādāya pakkamituṃ. So saṅghaṃ ticīvarena avippavāsasammutiṃ yācati. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissassa) bhikkhuno ticīvarena avippavāsasammutiṃ dadey‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, ayaṃ (tisso) bhikkhu gilāno, na sakkoti ticīvaraṃ ādāya pakkamituṃ. So saṅghaṃ ticīvarena avippavāsasammutiṃ yācati. Saṅgho (tissassa) bhikkhuno ticīvarena avippavāsasammutiṃ deti. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno ticīvarena avippavāsasammutiyā dānaṃ, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Dinnā saṅghena (tissassa) bhikkhuno ticīvarena avippavāsasammuti. Khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī, evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
