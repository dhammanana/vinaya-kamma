Authorization for New Construction.

When a faithful person builds or causes a dwelling or monastery to be built, the bhikkhus should give advice to the donor for their convenience. Since not everyone can provide such advice, the Community should select a suitable bhikkhu for that task and grant him the 'Authorization for New Construction'. The bhikkhu who has received the authorization should advise the donor and the workers so that the monastery construction is completed quickly and the work is carried out appropriately.

When the Great Lay-Devotee Visakha was building the Pubbarama Great Monastery, the Venerable Moggallana the Great organized the work. The construction of the Pubbarama Monastery, which otherwise could not have been completed for many years, was finished in nine months. (Nowadays, certain over-learned individuals who do not know the Vinaya mislead the public by preaching falsely that building monasteries and temples is not work belonging to bhikkhus.)

The Procedural Text for Granting the Authorization for New Construction.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (itthannāmassa) gahapatino vihāraṃ (itthannāmassa) bhikkhuno navakammaṃ dadey‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, (itthannāmassa) gahapatino vihāraṃ (itthannāmassa) bhikkhuno navakammaṃ deti. Yassāyasmato khamati (itthannāmassa) gahapatino vihāraṃ (itthannāmassa) bhikkhuno navakammassa dānaṃ. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Dinno saṅghena (itthannāmassa) gahapatino vihāro (itthannāmassa) bhikkhuno navakammaṃ. Khamati saṅghassa. Tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
