The Person Suitable for Bestowing the Kathina Cloth

Not all are suitable for bestowing the Kathina cloth. “Aṭṭhahaṃgehi samannāgato puggalo bhabbo kaṭhinaṃ attharituṃ. Katamehi aṭṭhahaṃgehi? Pubbakaraṇaṃ jānāti. Paccuddhāraṃ jānāti. Adhiṭṭhānaṃ jānāti. Atthāraṃ jānāti. Māṭikaṃ jānāti. Paḷibodhaṃ jānāti. Uddhāraṃ jānāti. Ānisaṃsaṃ jānāti. Imehi aṭṭhahaṃgehi samannāgato puggalo bhabbo kaṭhinaṃ attharituṃ”

Thus, in the Parivārapāḷi, it is declared that only a bhikkhu endowed with the eight factors—knowing the preliminary act, knowing the withdrawal, knowing the determination, knowing the spreading, knowing the matrix, knowing the impediments, knowing the removal, and knowing the advantages—is suitable for spreading the Kathina. The eight points mentioned for a person to be suitable for receiving a Kathina must be learned by the bhikkhus.
