Matters to be known concerning the Community's Uposatha

Since it has been declared: “Na bhikkhave! Saṅghamajjhe anajjhiṭṭhena pātimokkhaṃ uddisitabbaṃ. Yo uddiseyya. Āpatti dukkaṭassa”, after the Community of monks has assembled within the sīmā, for the recitation of the Pātimokkha, an invitation should be made by a bhikkhu who has received Authorization, either to the senior thera of the Community or to another thera skilled in Vinaya. The thera who has received the invitation, before reciting the Pātimokkha, should question the bhikkhu who made the invitation concerning the Vinaya. This too should be done after obtaining Authorization. The bhikkhu who made the invitation should also resolve the Vinaya questions after obtaining Authorization. 'Authorization to Question the Vinaya and Authorization to Answer' can also be obtained by oneself. Obtaining it by oneself is the current custom. The method of making invitations, etc., will be shown below. Therein, the sentences will be presented using the names Tissa, Datta. When performing the Uposatha, the names of the respective individuals should be used in those places.
