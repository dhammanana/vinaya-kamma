Adhiṭṭhāna Pavāraṇā

If, on the day of Pavāraṇā, there is only one bhikkhu, that bhikkhu should go to the sīmā, sweep, set out water, arrange seats, light lamps, and sit there. If, while he is sitting, other bhikkhus arrive, he should perform Pavāraṇā with those bhikkhus. If they do not arrive, he should say “Ajja me pavāraṇā (paṇṇarasīti) adhiṭṭhāmi” three times. This is Adhiṭṭhāna Pavāraṇā.
