The Act of Censure (Tajjanīya Kamma)

During the time of the Buddha, the two bhikkhus named Paṇḍuka and Lohitaka were frequently makers of strife, makers of quarrels, makers of disputes, makers of idle talk, and makers of legal questions in the Community, and they exhorted other bhikkhus to do the same. To discipline such bhikkhus, the Blessed One prescribed this Act of Censure (Tajjanīya Kamma). When performing it, bring the quarrelsome bhikkhus into the midst of the Community, charge them, remind them of the offense, and having made them acknowledge the offense, the formal act should be performed.

The formal text of the motion is as follows:

Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho, ime (Paṇḍuka Lohitakā) bhikkhū attanā bhaṇḍanakārakā kalahakārakā vivādakārakā bhassakārakā saṅghe adhikaraṇakārakā, yepi caññe bhikkhū bhaṇḍanakārakā kalahakārakā vivādakārakā bhassakārakā saṅghe adhikaraṇakārakā. Te upasaṅkamitvā evaṃ vadanti “mā kho tumhe āyasmanto eso ajesi, balavā balavaṃ paṭimantetha, tumhe tena paṇḍitatarā ca byattatarā ca bahussutatarā ca alamattatarā ca, mā cassa bhāyittha. Mayampi tumhākaṃ pakkhā bhavissāmā”ti. Tena anuppannāni ceva bhaṇḍanāni uppajjanti uppannāni ca bhaṇḍanāni bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattanti. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīyakammaṃ kareyya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho, ime (Paṇḍuka Lohitakā) bhikkhū attanā bhaṇḍana kārakā -pe- saṅgho adhikaraṇa kārakā, yepi caññe bhikkhū bhaṇḍanakārakā -pe- saṅghe adhikaraṇakārakā, te upasaṅkamitvā evaṃ vadanti “mā kho tumhe āyasmanto eso ajesi, balavā balavaṃ paṭimantetha tumhe tena paṇḍitatarā ca byattatarā ca bahussutatarā ca alamattatarā ca mā cassa bhāyittha, mayampi tumhākaṃ pakkhā bhavissāmā” ti tena anuppannāni ca bhaṇḍanāni uppajjanti. Uppannāni ca bhaṇḍanāni bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattanti. Saṅgho (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīya kammaṃ karoti. Yassāyasmato khamati (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīya kammassa karaṇaṃ. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati. So bhāseyya.

Dutiyampi etamatthaṃ vadāmi. Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho, ime (Paṇḍuka Lohitakā) bhikkhū attanā bhaṇḍanakārakā -pe- saṅghe adhikaraṇakārakā. Ye’pi caññe bhikkhū bhaṇḍanakārakā -pe- saṅghe adhikaraṇakārakā, te upasaṅkamitvā evaṃ vadanti balavā balavaṃ paṭimantetha, tumhe tena paṇḍitatarā ca byattatarā ca bahussutatarā ca alamattatarā ca, mā cassa bhāyittha, mayampi tumhākaṃ pakkhā bhavissāmā”ti. Tena anuppannāni ceva bhaṇḍanāni uppajjanti. Uppannāni ca bhaṇḍanāni bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattanti. Saṅgho (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīyakammaṃ karoti yassāyasmato khamati (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīya kammassa karaṇaṃ. So tuṇhassa, yassa nakkhamati, so bhāseyya.

Tatiyampi etamatthaṃ vadāmi. Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho, ime (Paṇḍuka Lohitakā) bhikkhū attanā bhaṇḍanakārakā -pe- saṅghe adhikaraṇakārakā, ye’pi caññe bhikkhū bhaṇḍanakārakā -pe- saṅghe adhikaraṇakārakā te upasaṅkamitvā evaṃ vadanti “mā kho tumhe āyasmanto eso ajesi, balavā balavaṃ paṭimantetha tumhe tena paṇḍitatarā ca byattatarā ca bahussutatarā ca alamattatarā ca. Mā cassa bhāyittha, mayampi tumhākaṃ pakkhā bhavissāmā”ti tena anuppannāni ceva bhaṇḍanāni uppajjanti. Uppannāni ca bhaṇḍanāni bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattanti. Saṅgho (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīya kammaṃ karoti. Yassāyasmato khamati. (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīyakammassa karaṇaṃ, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati. So bhāseyya.

Kataṃ saṅghena (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīya kammaṃ. Khamati saṅghassa. Tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.

The Eighteen Duties to be Observed by Bhikkhus Subjected to the Act of Censure

A bhikkhu who has been subjected to the Act of Censure:

  1. Should not confer the full admission (Upasampadā) as a Preceptor or as a Teacher.
  2. Should not give the going-forth (Pabbajja) to a youth as a Preceptor.
  3. Should not give dependence (Nissaya) to other bhikkhus.
  4. Should not accept the agreement to exhort the bhikkhunis.
  5. Even if he has previously received the agreement, he should not exhort the bhikkhunis.
  6. He should not commit that same offense for which the Act of Censure was performed.
  7. He should not commit another offense of a similar kind.
  8. He should not commit any worse offense.
  9. He should not criticize the Act of Censure.
  10. He should not criticize those bhikkhus who performed the formal act.
  11. He should not suspend the Uposatha of a regular bhikkhu.
  12. He should not suspend the Pavarana of a regular bhikkhu.
  13. He should not issue a summons to a regular bhikkhu.
  14. He should not take precedence in the monastery.
  15. He should not ask for leave from a regular bhikkhu to charge him.
  16. He should not charge a regular bhikkhu.
  17. He should not remind a regular bhikkhu of an offense.
  18. He should not incite bhikkhus to quarrel.

Revocation of the Act of Censure

If the bhikkhus subjected to the Act of Censure conduct themselves rightly by fulfilling the aforementioned eighteen duties and request the Community to revoke the act, the Community should revoke the Act of Censure. Those bhikkhus should approach the Community, arrange their upper robes over one shoulder, pay homage at the feet of the senior bhikkhus, sit in the squatting position, and with joined palms, request the revocation of the Act of Censure by reciting the following passage three times:

Mayaṃ bhante saṅghena tajjanīyakammakatā. Sammā vattāma. Lomaṃ pātema. Netthāraṃ vattāma. Tajjanīyakammassa paṭippassaddhiṃ yācāma. Dutiyampi -pe- Tatiyampi -pe-

The formal text for Revocation:

Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho. Ime (Paṇḍuka Lohitakā) bhikkhū saṅghena tajjanīyakammakkatā. Sammā vattanti. Lomaṃ pātenthi. Netthāraṃ vattanti. Tajjanīyakammassa paṭippassaddhiṃ yācanti. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīyakammaṃ paṭippassambheyya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho. Ime (Paṇḍuka Lohitakā) bhikkhū saṅghena tajjanīyakammakkatā. Sammā vattanti. Lomaṃ pātenthi. Netthāraṃ vattanti. Tajjanīyakammassa paṭippassaddhiṃ yācanti. Saṅgho (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīya kammaṃ paṭippassambheti. Yassāyasmato khamati. (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīya kammassa paṭippassaddhi, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāseyya.

Dutiyampi etamatthaṃ vadāmi. Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho. Ime (Paṇḍuka Lohitakā) -pe- saṅgho (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīyakammaṃ paṭippassambheti. Yassāyasmato khamati (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīya kammassa paṭippassambheti, yassāyasmato khamati (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīyakammassa paṭippassaddhi. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāseyya.

Tatiyampi etamatthaṃ vadāmi. Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho. Ime (Paṇḍuka Lohitakā) bhikkhū -pe- tajjanīyakammassa paṭippassaddhiṃ yācanti. Saṅgho (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīyakammaṃ paṭippassambheti. Yassāyasmato khamati (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīyakammassa paṭippassaddhi, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāseyya.

Paṭippassaddhaṃ saṅghena (Paṇḍuka Lohitakānaṃ) bhikkhūnaṃ tajjanīyakammaṃ khamati saṅghassa. Tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
