Pārisuddhi Uposatha.

The Uposatha performed when a sufficient number of bhikkhus for the aforementioned Sangha Uposatha, or Suttuddesa Uposatha, have not assembled within the sīmā, but only three or two bhikkhus have assembled within the sīmā, is the Pārisuddhi Uposatha. If three bhikkhus have assembled within the sīmā, having performed the preliminary duties and preliminary acts, one bhikkhu shall lay the Motion thus: “Suṇantu me āyasmantā ajjuposatho (paṇṇaraso, cātuddiso) yadāyasmantānaṃ pattakallaṃ mayaṃ aññamaññaṃ pārisuddhi uposathaṃ karey‍yāma”. First, the senior bhikkhu, having arranged his outer robe over one shoulder, sat in the squatting posture, and paid homage, should say three times: “Parisuddho ahaṃ āvuso parisuddhoti maṃ dhāretha”. The remaining two should likewise say three times: “Parisuddho ahaṃ bhante parisuddhoti maṃ dhāretha”.

If two bhikkhus have assembled within the sīmā, having completed all preliminary acts, without laying a Motion, they should declare their purity to each other as stated.

Furthermore, on the day of the Great Pavāraṇā, when bhikkhus who have observed the Rains-residence earlier are performing Pavāraṇā, bhikkhus who have observed the Rains-residence later, and bhikkhus who have not observed the Rains-residence, and bhikkhus whose Rains-residence was broken, should participate in the Pavāraṇā Monastic Procedure by giving their bodily presence, and declare their purity by saying: “Parisuddho ahaṃ bhante parisuddhoti maṃ dhāretha”. When bhikkhus who have observed the Rains-residence later are performing Pavāraṇā, those who observed it earlier, and those who did not observe it, and those whose Rains-residence was broken, should declare their purity as stated. Furthermore, after the recitation of the Pātimokkha has concluded, before those bhikkhus depart from the sīmā, bhikkhus who arrived later should also declare their purity to the bhikkhus who performed the Suttuddesa Uposatha.
