Authorization of an Assigner of Lodgings.

In monasteries belonging to the Community where many bhikkhus arrive and reside, the Community should appoint a bhikkhu to assign lodgings—that is, to designate specific lodgings for arriving bhikkhus. That bhikkhu should count the bhikkhus and the lodgings, and then appropriately assign the bhikkhus to those respective lodgings.

The Formal Act for the Authorization of an Assigner of Lodgings.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ senāsanagāhāpakaṃ sammanney‍ya, esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ senāsanagāhāpakaṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato. Khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno senāsanagāhāpakassa sammuti. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya. Sammato saṅghena (tiso) bhikkhu senāsanagāhāpako. Khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
