Authorization of a Distributor of Trifles.

During times when the Community's stores do not receive enough requisites to be distributed among all the bhikkhus, the Tathagata authorized the 'Authorization of a Distributor of Trifles' to appoint a bhikkhu to distribute small items required by individual bhikkhus. A bhikkhu who has received this authorization has the authority to distribute small items from the stores without consulting the Community. Such a bhikkhu should give a needle to a bhikkhu requesting one for sewing robes. He should give a small knife to one who asks for a knife. He should give a pair of sandals to a bhikkhu departing on a long journey. He should give a waist-band to a bhikkhu requesting one. He should give a shoulder-strap to a bhikkhu who needs one. He should give a water-filter to a bhikkhu who needs one. He should give a bowl-bag to a bhikkhu who needs one. He should give cloth to bhikkhus requesting it for long panels, cross panels, circles, half-circles, or borders. He should give ghee, honey, and such items in an amount sufficient for a single serving.

The Formal Act.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ appamattakavissajjakaṃ sammanney‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ appamattakavissajjakaṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno appamattakavissajjakassa sammuti. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Sammato saṅghena (tisso) bhikkhu appamattakavissajjako, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
