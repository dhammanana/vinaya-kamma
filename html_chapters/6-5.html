The Adhitthāna Uposatha.

If, on the Uposatha day, only one bhikkhu is present, having gone to the sīmā and completed all preliminary duties, and considering that no other bhikkhus will arrive to perform the Uposatha, if it is a fourteenth-day Uposatha, he should recite three times: “Ajja me cātuddaso uposathoti adhiṭṭhāmi”; or if it is a fifteenth-day Uposatha, he should recite three times: “Ajja me uposatho paṇṇarasoti adhiṭṭhāmi”. This is called the Adhitthāna Uposatha.
