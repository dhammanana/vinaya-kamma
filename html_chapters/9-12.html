Monastic Procedure for Removing the Kathina

If the Kathina has been spread in a certain monastery, all robes arising in that monastery until the end of the winter season belong to the bhikkhus who spread the Kathina. They do not belong to others. Therefore, if some faithful person wishes to offer many robes to the Saṅgha so that they may also belong to other bhikkhus, and requests the Saṅgha to perform the removal of the Kathina, the Saṅgha must perform the removal of the Kathina. Thereafter, all robes received by that monastery must be distributed equally among all bhikkhus.
