Authorization of a Disposer of Money

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ rūpiyachaḍḍhakaṃ sammanney‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ rūpiyachaḍḍhakaṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno rūpiyachaḍḍhakassa sammuti, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Sammato saṅghena (tisso) bhikkhu rūpīyachaḍḍhako. Khamati saṅghassa. Tasmā tuṇahī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
