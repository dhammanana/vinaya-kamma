Authorization of a Receiver of Bathing Cloths

Since the bathing cloths received by the Community must be distributed appropriately, the Blessed One authorized the Authorization of a Receiver of Bathing Cloths.

The procedural text is as follows:

Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (Tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ sāṭiyagāhāpakaṃ sammanneyya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! Saṅgho, saṅgho (Tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ sāṭiyagāhāpakaṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato khamati (Tissassa) bhikkhuno sāṭiyagāhāpakassa sammuti, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāseyya.

Sammato saṅghena (Tisso) bhikkhu sāṭiyagāhāpako. Khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
