Rehabilitation (Osāraṇa)

If a novice who has been expelled has reformed, abandoned his wrong views, and asks the Community for forgiveness, have him request the removal of the act of expulsion three times. He should then be rehabilitated by an act of announcement (apalokana-kamma).

The Act-Text

Saṅghaṃ bhante! āpucchāmi. Ayaṃ (kaṇṭhako) sāmaṇero buddhassa dhammassa saṅghassa avaṇṇavādī micchādiṭṭhiko. Yaṃ aññe sāmaṇerā labhanti dviratta tirattaṃ bhikkhūhi saddhiṃ sahasey‍yaṃ, tassā alābhāya nissārito. Svāyaṃ idāni orato virato lajjidhammaṃ okkanto, hirottappe patiṭṭhito, katadaṇḍa kammo, accayaṃ deseti. Imassa sāmaṇerassa yathā pure kāyasambhoga sāmaggidānaṃ. Ruccati saṅghassa.
