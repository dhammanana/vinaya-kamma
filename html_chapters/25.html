The Act of Turning Over the Alms-bowl (Pattanikkujjana Kamma)

The Act of Turning Over the Alms-bowl is a formal act performed as a penalty for laypeople who have wronged bhikkhus. Its purpose is the boycotting of the offending layperson by the Community. A layperson for whom the bowl has been turned over must be avoided by the bhikkhus. No bhikkhu should accept anything offered by him. Even when wandering for alms, his house should be passed by. This act was instituted on account of the Licchavi named Vaddha, who brought a false accusation of a breach of morality against the Elder Dabba Mallaputta.

Aṭṭhahi bhikkhave! aṅgehi samannāgatassa upāsakassa patto nikukjjitabbo. Bhikkhūnaṃ alābhāya parisakkati. Bhikkhūnaṃ anat‍thāya parisakkati. Bhikkhūnaṃ avāsāya parisakkati. Bhikkhūnaṃ akkosati paribhāsati bhikkhu bhikkhūhi bhedeti. Buddhassa avaṇṇaṃ bhāsati. Dhammassa avaṇṇaṃ bhāsati. Saṅghassa avaṇṇaṃ bhāsati. Anujānāmi bhikkhave! imehi aṭṭhahaṅgehi samannāgatassa upāsakassa pattaṃ nikkujjituṃ.

As declared by the Blessed One, the Act of Turning Over the Alms-bowl should be performed against a lay follower who possesses these eight qualities: he strives for the loss of gains for bhikkhus; he strives for the harm of bhikkhus; he strives for the lack of residence for bhikkhus; he reviles and insults bhikkhus; he causes dissension among bhikkhus; he speaks in dispraise of the Buddha; he speaks in dispraise of the Dhamma; he speaks in dispraise of the Sangha.

The Formal Act for Turning Over the Alms-bowl

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho. (vaḍḍho licchavī) āyasmantaṃ (dabbaṃ mallaputtaṃ) amūlikāya sīlavipattiyā anuddhaṃseti. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ. Saṅgho (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) pattaṃ nikkujjey‍ya asambhogaṃ saṅghena karey‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho. (vaḍḍho licchavī) āyasmantaṃ (dabbaṃ mallaputtaṃ) amūlikāya sīlavipattiyā anuddhaṃseti. Saṅgho (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) pattaṃ nikkujjati, sambhogaṃ saṅghena karoti. Yassāyasmato khamati (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) pattassa nikkujjanā asambhogaṃ saṅghena karaṇaṃ. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Nikkujjito saṅghena (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) patto asambhogo saṅghena, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.

The Removal of the Turning Over of the Alms-bowl

If the person against whom the Act of Turning Over the Alms-bowl was performed desires its removal, have him acknowledge the fault and obtain forgiveness from the Community. Have him approach the Community, arrange his upper robe over one shoulder, pay homage at the feet of the bhikkhus, sit on his haunches, and with hands joined in reverence, request the turning upright of the alms-bowl by reciting this formula:

Saṅghena me bhante! patto nikkujjito, asambhogomhi saṅghena, so’haṃ bhante sammā vattāmi. Lomaṃ pātemi. Netthāraṃ vattāmi, saṅghaṃ pattukkujjanaṃ yācāmi. Dutiyampi -pe- tatiyampi -pe-

The Formal Act for Turning the Alms-bowl Upright

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho. Saṅghena (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) patto nikkujjito asambhogo saṅghena, so sammā vattati. Lomaṃ pāteti. Net‍thāraṃ vattati. Saṅghaṃ pattakkujjanaṃ yācati. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ. Saṅgho (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) pattaṃ ukkujjey‍ya. Sambhogaṃ saṅghena karey‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho. Saṅghena (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) patto nikkujjito asambhogo saṅghena, so sammā vattati. Lomaṃ pāteti. Net‍thāraṃ vattati. Saṅghaṃ pattukkajjanaṃ yācati. Saṅgho (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) pattaṃ ukkujjati, sambhogaṃ saṅghena karoti. Yassāyasmato khamati (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) pattassa ukkujjanā sambhogaṃ saṅghena karaṇaṃ, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Ukkujjito saṅghena (vaḍḍhassa licchavissa) patto, sambhogo saṅghena, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
