Authorization of a Storehouse.

At that time, the bhikkhus who were looking after the robes had no designated place to keep them, and so they kept the robes in various places. Mice ate those robes. Termites also ate them. When this matter was reported to the Blessed One, he allowed the authorization of a storehouse.

The Procedural Text.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (it‍thannāmaṃ) vihāraṃ bhaṇḍāgāraṃ sammanney‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, saṅgho (it‍thannāmaṃ) vihāraṃ bhaṇḍāgāraṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato khamati. (itthannāmassa) vihārassa bhaṇḍāgārassa sammuti, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Sammato saṅghena (it‍thannāmo) vihāro bhaṇḍāgāraṃ, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
