The Four Unperformables

Now, listen to the Four Unperformables.

(1) “Upasampannena bhikkhunā methuno dhammo na patisevitabbo, antamaso tiracchāna gatāyapi. Yo bhikkhu methunaṃ dhammaṃ patisevati, assamaṇo hoti asakyaputtiyo. Seyyathāpi nāma puriso sīsacchinno abhabbo tena sarīra bandhanena jīvituṃ. Evameva bhikkhu methunaṃ dhammaṃ patisevitvā assamaṇo hoti asakyaputtiyo. Taṃ te yāvajīvaṃ akaraṇīyaṃ”

By a bhikkhu who has received Higher Ordination, sexual intercourse should not be engaged in, not even with an animal. If any bhikkhu engages in sexual intercourse, by that he is no recluse, no son of the Sakyan. Just as a man whose head has been cut off is incapable of living again by that body connection, even so, a bhikkhu who has engaged in sexual intercourse is no recluse, no son of the Sakyan. Therefore, by you, that sexual intercourse should not be done as long as life lasts.

(2) “Upasampannena bhikkhunā adinnaṃ theyya saṅkhātaṃ na ādātabbaṃ antamaso tiṇasalākaṃ upādāya. Yo bhikkhu pādaṃ vā pādārahaṃ vā atirekapādaṃ vā adinnaṃ theyyasaṃkhātaṃ ādiyati assamaṇo hoti asakyaputtiyo. Seyyathāpi nāma paṇḍupalāso bandhanā pamutto abhabbo haritatthāya. Evameva bhikkhu pādaṃ vā pādārahaṃ vā atirekapādaṃ vā adinnaṃ theyyasaṃkhātaṃ ādiyitvā assamaṇo hoti asakyaputtiyo. Taṃ te yāvajīvaṃ akaraṇīyaṃ.”

By a bhikkhu who has received Higher Ordination, from a blade of grass upwards, that which is not given by the owners should not be taken with a thievish intent. If any bhikkhu takes with a thievish intent a *pada* of wealth, or an object worth a *pada*, or an object worth more than a *pada*, he is no recluse, no son of the Sakyan. Just as a withered leaf detached from its stalk is incapable of becoming green again, even so, a bhikkhu who has taken with a thievish intent a *pada* of wealth, or an object worth a *pada*, or an object worth more than a *pada*, is no recluse, no son of the Sakyan. That, by you, should not be done as long as life lasts.

(3) “Upasampannena bhikkhunā sañcicca pāṇo jīvitā na voropetabbo antamaso kuntha kipillikaṃ upādāya. Yo bhikkhu sañcicca manussaviggahaṃ jīvitā voropeti. Antamaso gabbha pātanaṃ upādāya. Assamaṇo hoti asakyaputtiyo. Seyyathāpi nāma puthusīlā dvidhā bhinnā appaṭisandhikā hoti. Evameva bhikkhu sañcicca manussa viggahaṃ jīvitā voropetvā assamaṇo hoti asakyaputtiyo. Taṃ te yāvajīvaṃ akaraṇīyaṃ”

By a bhikkhu who has received Higher Ordination, from an ant upwards, a living being should not be intentionally deprived of life. If any bhikkhu intentionally deprives a human being of life, even by causing an abortion, that bhikkhu is no recluse, no son of the Sakyan. Just as a slab of rock split in two is incapable of being joined again, even so, a bhikkhu who has intentionally deprived a human being of life is no recluse, no son of the Sakyan. That, by you, should not be done as long as life lasts.

(4) “Upasampannena bhikkhunā uttarimanussa dhammo na ullapitabbo antamaso suññāgāre abhiramāmīti. Yo bhikkhu pāpiccho icchāpakato asantaṃ abhūtaṃ uttarimanussadhammaṃ ullapati jhānaṃ vā vimokkhaṃ vā samādhiṃ vā samāpattiṃ vā maggaṃ vā phalaṃ vā. Assamaṇo hoti. Asakya puttiyo. Seyyathāpi nāma tālo matthakacchinno abhabbo puna virūḷhiyā. Evameva bhikkhu pāpiccho icchāpakato asantaṃ abhūtaṃ uttarīmanussadhammaṃ ullapitvā assamaṇo hoti. Asakyaputtiyo. Taṃ te yāvajīvaṃ akaraṇīyaṃ”

By a bhikkhu who has received Higher Ordination, even by saying, “I dwell delighting in an empty dwelling,” a superhuman state should not be declared. If any bhikkhu, being of evil wishes, overcome by evil wishes, declares a non-existent, unreal superhuman state in himself, such as jhāna, or vimokkha, or samādhi, or samāpatti, or magga, or phala, by that he is no recluse, no son of the Sakyan. Just as a palm tree whose crown has been cut off is incapable of growing again, even so, a bhikkhu, being of evil wishes, overcome by evil wishes, having declared a non-existent superhuman state in himself, is no recluse, no son of the Sakyan. That, by you, should not be done as long as life lasts.
