The Motion for Pavāraṇā

“Suṇātu me bhante saṅgho. Ajja pavāraṇā paṇṇarasī. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho tevācikaṃ pavārey‍ya.”

After the Motion has been laid thus, the senior bhikkhu of the Community, having arranged his robe over one shoulder, sitting in the squatting posture, and having paid homage, [should say]:-

“Saṅghaṃ avuso pavāremi. Diṭṭhena vā sutena vā parisaṃkāya vā vadantu maṃ Āyasmanto anukampaṃ upādāya passanto paṭikarissāmi. Dutiyampi āvuso saṅghaṃ pavāremi -pe- tatiyampi āvuso saṅghaṃ pavāremi -pe- passanto paṭikarissāmi.”

Pavāraṇā should be performed thus. When the senior bhikkhu of the Community performs Pavāraṇā, the others should not sit on their seats, but should sit in the squatting posture. After the Great Elder has performed Pavāraṇā, the remaining bhikkhus should perform Pavāraṇā in order of seniority. The junior bhikkhus should remain in the squatting posture until the senior bhikkhus have performed Pavāraṇā. After each has performed his own Pavāraṇā, it is permissible to sit on one's seat. The newly ordained bhikkhus should perform Pavāraṇā thus:

Saṅghaṃ bhante pavāremi. Diṭṭhena vā sutena vā parisaṃkāya vā vadantu maṃ āyasmanto anukampaṃ upādāya passanto paṭikarissāmi. Dutiyampi bhante saṅghaṃ -pe- tatiyampi bhante saṅghaṃ -pe- paṭikarissāmi.

Among the Community performing Pavāraṇā, if there are bhikkhus who have observed the Rains Retreat later, or who have not observed the Rains Retreat, or whose Rains Retreat has been broken, they should announce their purity. If there are bhikkhus who observed the Rains Retreat earlier at a place where bhikkhus who observed the Rains Retreat later are performing Pavāraṇā, they too should announce their purity.

On the day of the Great Pavāraṇā, if among the bhikkhus assembled within a sīmā, those who observed the Rains Retreat later are numerous, the Motion for Pavāraṇā should not be laid, but the Pātimokkha should be recited. Thereafter, without laying a Motion, those who observed the Rains Retreat earlier should perform Pavāraṇā to those bhikkhus. If both groups are equal in number, the Motion for Pavāraṇā should be laid, and those who observed the Rains Retreat earlier should perform Pavāraṇā. Those who observed the Rains Retreat later should announce their purity to those bhikkhus. The same procedure should be followed on the day of the later Rains Retreat Pavāraṇā. If on the day of the Great Pavāraṇā, only two bhikkhus, one who observed the Rains Retreat earlier and one who observed it later, have gathered in one sīmā, the bhikkhu who observed the Rains Retreat earlier should perform Pavāraṇā to the other bhikkhu. The bhikkhu who observed the Rains Retreat later should announce his purity.
