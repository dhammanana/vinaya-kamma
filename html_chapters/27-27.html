Authorization for the Insane.

For a Monastic Procedure to be performed, all bhikkhus residing within the boundary must be present. The consent of bhikkhus who cannot attend must be taken. In the time of the Buddha, a bhikkhu named Gagga, who was insane, would attend the Monastic Procedure if he wished, and would not attend if he did not wish. This became a trouble for the Community. In order to eliminate the trouble caused to the Community by insane bhikkhus, the Tathagata authorized the “Authorization for the Insane” for insane bhikkhus.

The Formal Statement.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, (gaggo) bhikkhu ummattako, sarati’pi uposathaṃ na’pi sarati, sarati’pi saṅghakammaṃ na’pi sarati āgacchati’pi uposathaṃ na’pi āgacchati, āgacchati’pi saṅghakammaṃ na’pi āgacchati. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (gaggassa) bhikkhuno ummattakassa ummattakasammutiṃ dadey‍ya. Sarey‍ya vā (gaggo) bhikkhu upeāsathaṃ na vā sarey‍ya. Sarey‍ya vā saṅghakammaṃ na vā sarey‍ya, āgacchey‍ya vā uposathaṃ na vā āgacchey‍ya, āgacchey‍ya vā saṅghakammaṃ na vā āgacchey‍ya, saṅgho saha vā (gaggena) vinā vā (gaggena) uposathaṃ karey‍ya. Saṅghakammaṃ karey‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, (gaggo) bhikkhu ummattako sarati’pi uposathaṃ na’pisarati sarati’pi saṅghakammaṃ na’pi sarati, āgacchati’pi uposathaṃ na’pi. Āgacchati. Āgacchati’pi saṅghakammaṃ na’pi āgacchati, saṅgho (gaggassa) bhikkhuno ummattakassa ummattaka sammutiṃ deti. Sarey‍ya vā (gaggo) bhikkhu uposathaṃ na vā sarey‍ya, sarey‍ya vā saṅghakammaṃ na vā āgacchey‍ya, saṅgho saha vā (gaggena) vinā vā (gaggena) uposathaṃ karissati, saṅghakammaṃ karissati, yassāyasmato khamati (gaggassa) bhikkhuno ummattakassa ummattakasammutiyā dānaṃ. Sarey‍ya vā (gaggo) bhikkhu uposathaṃ na vā sarey‍ya, sarey‍ya vā saṅghakammaṃ na vā sarey‍ya, āgacchey‍ya vā uposathaṃ na vā āgacchey‍ya, āgacchey‍ya vā saṅghakammaṃ na vā āgacchey‍ya, saṅgho saha vā (gaggena) vinā vā (gaggena) uposathaṃ karissati, saṅghakammaṃ karissati, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Dinnā saṅghena (gaggassa) bhikkhuno ummattakassa ummattaka sammuti, sarey‍ya vā (gaggo) bhikkhu uposathaṃ na vā sarey‍ya, sarey‍ya vā saṅghakammaṃ na vā sarey‍ya, āgacchey‍ya vā uposathaṃ na vā āgacchey‍ya, āgacchey‍ya vā saṅghakammaṃ na vā āgacchey‍ya, saṅgho saha vā (gaggena) vinā vā (gaggena) uposathaṃ karissati. Saṅghakammaṃ karissati, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.

After the Authorization for the Insane has been granted to an insane bhikkhu in this manner, whether that bhikkhu is present in the midst of the Community or outside the boundary of the Community, the Monastic Procedure performed thereby is not harmed.
