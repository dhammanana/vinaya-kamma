Authorization of a Robe-Depositor.

At that time, the bhikkhus who had received the authorization to receive robes, having received the robes, would leave them lying about here and there. Consequently, the robes belonging to the Community were being lost. When the bhikkhus informed the Blessed One of this matter, he allowed the Community to authorize a bhikkhu endowed with five qualities as a robe-depositor.

The Formal Motion.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅ‍gho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ cīvaranidahakaṃ sammanney‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ cīvaranidahakaṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno cīvaranidahakassa sammuti. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya,

Sammato saṅghena (tisso) bhikkhu cīvaranidahako, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
