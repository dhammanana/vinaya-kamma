Formal Act for the Authorization of a Distributor of Rice-gruel.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ yāgubhājakaṃ sammanney‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ yāgubhājakaṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno yāgubhājakassa sammuti. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Sammato saṅghena (tisso) bhikkhu yāgubhājako, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
