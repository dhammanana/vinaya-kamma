Pavāraṇā of a Group of Four

If the assembly gathered within the sīmā on the day of Pavāraṇā consists of four bhikkhus, each bhikkhu should perform Pavāraṇā before the remaining three. This is a group Pavāraṇā. It must be performed by establishing a Motion. The Motion is as follows:

Suṇantu me āyasmanto ajja pavāraṇā (paṇṇarasī) yadāyasmantānaṃ pattakallaṃ mayaṃ aññamaññaṃ pavārey‍yāma.

Having established the Motion thus, the senior bhikkhu, having draped his robes over one shoulder, squatting, and having paid homage, [should perform Pavāraṇā]:

“Ahaṃ āvuso āyasmante pavāremi. Diṭṭhena vā sutena vā parisaṅkāya vā vadantu maṃ āyasmanto anukampaṃ upādāya passanto paṭikarissāmi. Dutiyampi ahaṃ āvuso -pe- tatiyampi ahaṃ āvuso āyasmante pavāremi -pe- passanto paṭikarissāmi” Thus

Pavāraṇā should be performed. By the junior bhikkhus -

“Ahaṃ bhante āyasmante pavāremi. Diṭṭhena vā sutena vā parisaṅkāya vā vadantu maṃ āyasmanto anukampaṃ upādāya passanto paṭikarissāmi. Dutiyampi -pe- tatiyampi -pe- paṭikarissāmi” Thus Pavāraṇā should be performed.
