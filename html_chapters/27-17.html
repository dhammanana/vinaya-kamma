Formal Act for the Appointment of a Distributor of Confectionery

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ khajjabhājakaṃ sammanney‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ khajjabhājakaṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno khajjabhājakassa sammuti, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Sammato saṅghena (tisso) bhikkhu khajjabhājako, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
