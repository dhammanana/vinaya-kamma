Authorization for a Sling.

The Authorization for a Sling is granted to weak, sick bhikkhus who find it difficult to carry their alms-bowl without hanging it in a sling. A bhikkhu who desires to receive this should approach the Community of monks, arrange his outer robe over one shoulder, bow at the feet of the senior bhikkhus, sit in a squatting position, and request the Authorization for a Sling from the Community with the following statement:

Ahaṃ bhante gilāno. Na sakkomi vinā sikkāya pattaṃ pariharituṃ, so’haṃ bhante saṅghaṃ sikkā sammutiṃ yācāmi. Dutiyampi -pe- tatiyampi -pe-

Monastic Procedure Text.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, ayaṃ (tisso) bhikkhu gilāno, na sakkoti vinā sikkāya pattaṃ pariharituṃ, so saṅghaṃ sikkāsammutiṃ yācati. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissassa) bhikkhuno sikkāsammutiṃ dadey‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, ayaṃ (tisso) bhikkhu gilāno, na sakkoti vinā sikkāya pattaṃ pariharituṃ, so saṅghaṃ sikkāsammutiṃ yācati. Saṅgho (tissassa) bhikkhuno sikkāsammutiṃ deti. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno sikkāsammutiyā dānaṃ. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Dinnā saṅghena (tissassa) bhikkhuno sikkāsammuti. Khamati saṅghassa. Tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
