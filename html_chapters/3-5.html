Higher Ordination of Two or Three Persons at Once

At that time, disputes arose among the Candidates for Higher Ordination regarding who should receive Higher Ordination first. When this was reported to the Buddha, He permitted the Higher Ordination of two or three persons with the same Preceptor by a single announcement, saying: “Anujānāmi bhikkhave dve tayo ekānusāvane kātuṃ. Tañca kho ekena upajjhāyena, natveva nānupajjhāyena”. However, the formal procedure for this is not found in the Vinaya Pali. In the formal procedure books of Burma, it is presented thus:

“Suṇātu me bhante saṅgho. Ayaṃ ca nāgo ayaṃ ca datto āyasmato tissassa upasampadāpekkhā, parisuddhā antarāyikehi dhammehi. Paripuṇṇimesaṃ pattacīvaraṃ. Nāgo ca datto ca saṅghaṃ upasampadaṃ yācanti. Āyasmatā tissena upajjhāyena. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ. Saṅgho nāgaṃ ca dattaṃ ca upasampādey‍ya āyasmatā tissena upajjhāyena. Esā ñatti.
Let the Sangha hear me, venerable sirs. This Candidate and this Datta are Candidates for Higher Ordination of the Venerable Tissa; they are pure of obstructing conditions. Their bowl and robes are complete. The Candidate and Datta request Higher Ordination from the Community, with the Venerable Tissa as Preceptor. If the Sangha is ready, the Community should confer Higher Ordination upon the Candidate and Datta, with the Venerable Tissa as Preceptor. This is the Motion.

Suṇātu me bhanto saṅgho. Ayaṃ ca nāgo ayaṃ ca datto āyasmato tissassa upasampadā pekkhā. Parisuddhā antarāyikehi dhammehi. Paripuṇṇimesaṃ pattacīvaraṃ. Nāgo ca datto ca saṅghaṃ upasampadaṃ yācanti. Āyasmatā tissena upajjhāyena. Saṅgho nāgaṃ ca dattaṃ ca upasampādeti. Āyasmatā tissena upajjhāyena. Yassāyasmato khamati nāgassa ca dattassa ca upasampadā. Āyasmatā. Tissena upajjhāyena. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati. So bhāsey‍ya.
Let the Sangha hear me, venerable sirs. This Candidate and this Datta are Candidates for Higher Ordination of the Venerable Tissa. They are pure of obstructing conditions. Their bowl and robes are complete. The Candidate and Datta request Higher Ordination from the Community, with the Venerable Tissa as Preceptor. The Community confers Higher Ordination upon the Candidate and Datta, with the Venerable Tissa as Preceptor. Whosoever of the venerable ones approves of the Higher Ordination of the Candidate and Datta, with the Venerable Tissa as Preceptor, should keep silent. Whosoever does not approve, should speak.

Dutiyampi etamat‍thaṃ vadāmi. -pe-
I state this matter a second time. -etc.-

Tatiyampi etamat‍thaṃ vadāmi. -pe- so bhāsey‍ya.
I state this matter a third time. -etc.- Whosoever does not approve, should speak.

Upasampannā saṅghena nāgo ca datto ca āyasmatā tissena upajjhāyena. Khamati saṅghassa. Tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.”
The Candidate and Datta have received Higher Ordination from the Community, with the Venerable Tissa as Preceptor. The Community approves. Therefore, it is Silent. Thus I bear this in mind.
