Authorization of an Assigner of Lodgings.

In monasteries where many bhikkhus frequently arrive, the Community should appoint a bhikkhu to assign lodgings. The bhikkhu who has received this authorization must appropriately assign lodgings to the bhikkhus arriving at the monastery.

The formal proclamation for the authorization of an Assigner of Lodgings.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ senāsanapaññāpakaṃ sammanney‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ senāsanapaññāpakaṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno senāsanapaññāpakassa sammuti, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Sammato saṅghena (tisso) bhikkhu senāsanapaññāpako, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.

The Arahant Dabba Mallaputta, son of the Malla kings, lived having received both the authorization of an Assigner of Lodgings and the authorization of a Distributor of Meals.
