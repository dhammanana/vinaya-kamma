Formal Act for the Authorization of a Fruit-Distributor

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ phalabhājakaṃ sammanney‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, saṅgho (tissaṃ) bhikkhuṃ phalabhājakaṃ sammannati. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno phalabhājakassa sammuti, so tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Sammato saṅghena (tisso) bhikkhu phalabhājako, khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
