Authorization for a Staff

A bhikkhu who is unable to walk without a staff must obtain the Authorization for a Staff in order to use one. The bhikkhu desiring it should approach the Community, arrange his upper robe over one shoulder, pay homage to the feet of the senior bhikkhus, sit in the squatting position, and with hands joined in reverence, say:

Ahaṃ bhante! gilāno, na sakkomi vinā daṇḍena āhiṇḍituṃ. So’haṃ bhante saṅghaṃ daṇḍasammutiṃ yācāmi. Dutiyampi -pe- tatiyampi -pe-

Having spoken these words, he should request the Authorization for a Staff from the Community.

The Procedural Text for Granting the Authorization for a Staff.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, ayaṃ (tisso) bhikkhu gilāno na sakkoti vinā daṇḍena āhiṇḍituṃ, so saṅghaṃ daṇḍasammutiṃ yācati. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṃ, saṅgho (tissassa) bhikkhuno daṇḍasammutiṃ dadey‍ya. Esā ñatti.

Suṇātu me bhante! saṅgho, ayaṃ (tisso) bhikkhu gilāno, na sakkoti vinā daṇḍena āhiṇḍituṃ, so saṅghaṃ daṇḍasammutiṃ yācati. Saṅgho (tissassa) bhikkhuno daṇḍasammutiṃ deti. Yassāyasmato khamati (tissassa) bhikkhuno daṇḍasammutiyā dānaṃ. So tuṇhassa. Yassa nakkhamati, so bhāsey‍ya.

Dinnā saṅghena (tissassa) bhikkhuno daṇḍa sammuti. Khamati saṅghassa, tasmā tuṇhī. Evametaṃ dhārayāmi.
