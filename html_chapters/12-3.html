A Matter to be Observed when Granting Probation and Penance.

In certain instances, owing to a shortage of bhikkhus, it may be necessary to include bhikkhus who have previously undertaken probation or penance in the proceedings for granting the same to others. In such circumstances, one must first relinquish the probation or penance; one should not participate in granting probation or penance to another bhikkhu while still observing it. If a bhikkhu who is observing probation or penance is counted toward the quorum of the Community, the probation or penance being granted is not validly effected. It is an invalid act. This applies similarly to the performance of the act of rehabilitation.

Mānattāraha catuttho ce bhikkhave! parivāsaṃ dadeyya, mūlāya paṭikasseyya, mānattaṃ dadeyya, vīso abbheyya, akammaṃ na ca karaṇīyaṃ. Mānattacārikacatuttho ce bhikkhave! parivāsaṃ dadeyya, mūlāya paṭikasseyya, mānattaṃ dadeyya. Vīso abbheyya, akammaṃ na ca karaṇīyaṃ, abbhānāraha catuttho ce bhikkhave! parivāsaṃ dadeyya, mūlāya paṭikasseyya, mānattaṃ dadeyya, vīso abbheyya. Akammaṃ na ca karaṇīyaṃ.

This is a teaching found in the Campeyya Khandhaka of the Mahāvagga Pāḷi.
