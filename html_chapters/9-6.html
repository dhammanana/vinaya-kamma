Inquiry into the Kathina

Just as in the Monastic Procedures of Uposatha and Pavāraṇā, there is a Vinaya inquiry to be made when conferring the Kathina. This is stated in the Vinayālaṅkāra thus:

“Kaṭhinadussaṃ labhitvā saṅghe sīmāya sannipatite ekena bhikkhunā bhante! saṅghassa idaṃ kaṭhinadussaṃ uppannaṃ saṅgho idaṃ kaṭhinadussaṃ itthaṃ nāmassa bhikkhuno dadey‍ya kaṭhinaṃ attharitunti vuttaṃ, aññena yo chiṇṇacīvaro tassāti vattabbaṃ. Puna purimena bahu chiṇṇacīvarāti vā natthi idha chiṇṇacīvaroti vā vutte, aparena tenahi vuḍḍhassāti vattabbaṃ. Puna purimena ko ettha vuḍḍhoti vutte, itarena itthaṃ nāmo bhikkhūti vattabbo. Puna purimena so bhikkhu tadaheva cīvaraṃ katvā attharituṃ sakkotīti vutte, aparena so sakkoti vā saṅgho mahātherassa saṅgahaṃ karissatītivā vattabbo. Puna purimena so mahāthero aṭṭhahi aṅgehi samannāgatoti vutte itarena āma samannāgatoti vattabbo. Tato sādhu suṭṭhu tassa dātabbanti vutte b‍yattena bhikkhunā paṭibalena saṅgho ñāpetabbo.”

The meaning is as follows:-

When the Kathina cloth has been received and the Community of monks has assembled within the sīmā, one bhikkhu should say: ‘Venerable Sirs, this Kathina cloth has arisen for the Community. May the Community give this Kathina cloth to such-and-such a bhikkhu for the purpose of spreading the Kathina.’ When this is said, another bhikkhu should say: ‘To the bhikkhu who has a worn-out robe.’ Again, when the first bhikkhu says: ‘There are many who have worn-out robes,’ or ‘There is no bhikkhu here who has a worn-out robe,’ the other bhikkhu should say: ‘Then to the senior bhikkhu.’ When the first bhikkhu says: ‘Who is the senior bhikkhu in this assembly?’ the other bhikkhu should say: ‘The bhikkhu named such-and-such.’ Again, when the first bhikkhu says: ‘Is that senior bhikkhu able to make a robe and spread the Kathina on this very day?’ the other bhikkhu should say: ‘He is able to do so,’ or ‘The Community will show favor to the Mahāthera.’ Again, when the first bhikkhu says: ‘Is that Mahāthera endowed with the eight qualities?’ the other bhikkhu should say: ‘Yes, he is endowed with the eight qualities.’ Thereupon, when it is said: ‘Good, it should be given to that bhikkhu,’ a competent and capable bhikkhu should inform the Community with the motion (ñatti) and confer the Kathina cloth upon that bhikkhu.
